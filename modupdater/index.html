Mod Updater

Automatically update all your Minecraft mods to the latest version via Modrinth's API. Just select your mods folder, pick your version and loader ➟ done.

  • Supports Fabric, Forge, NeoForge & Quilt
  • Skips mods that are already up to date
  • Detailed update log for easy troubleshooting
